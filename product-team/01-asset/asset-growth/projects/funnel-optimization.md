# Funnel Optimization

**Team:** Asset Growth
**PMM:** Maria Elisabeth Syauta
**Status:** Active

---

## Objective

Analyze and optimize the end-to-end app funnel to increase the number of unique traded assets per user. Users currently average 5 assets traded — the goal is to expand that by surfacing relevant assets and improving conversion at each funnel stage.

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Scope | Notes |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Monthly transacting users | Company-wide | Core product health metric |
| Trading volume | Company-wide | Tracked at company level |
| Unique traded assets per user | Asset Growth | Primary KPI — avg 5 assets/user as of 2025 |

---

## Funnel Stages

Full app funnel to be tracked and analyzed:

```
Acquisition → Activation → Revenue → Trade
```

Conversion drop-offs at each stage should be flagged and investigated.
